Definitions

For the purposes of these Terms:

    • Contestant

    “Contestant” is any person who
  • Sings and records a cover song based on the Corporate song of Siddhartha Bank Limited
  • Uploads the video in their YouTube channel (as deemed by Siddhartha Bank)
  • Contest Period shall mean the period commencing from 16th February, 2019 12AM to 2nd March, 2019 12 PM
  • Submits the link of the video in the given landing page, including their name, number and email

    • Song

  • “Song” here refers to the corporate theme song of the Siddhartha Bank Limited
  • “Covering” the song refers to singing the song and recording the same by the Contestant
  • The cover can be made with or without the use of any musical instruments and with or without the use of the backing track of the Siddhartha Bank corporate theme song
  • When someone choose to do a duet, or involve more than one singers, the owner of the YouTube channel will be chosen as the contestant

    • Sharing

  • The video should have at least 5000 views for it to be considered
  • The Contestant can use any social media platform to share the video from their YouTube channel in order to gather views on them

    • Winner

  • There will be only one Winner, who will be announced on 10th March, 209
  • Selection of the winner is solely based on the number of views on the Contestant’s YouTube video
  • The video with the most number of views by 12 PM of the Contest end date will be chosen as the winner, and thus the correspondent contestant
  • The winner will have to share his/her details via Direct Message (DM) within 10 Days of the announcement
  • In the event of the Winner being unreachable, ineligible, or fails to claim the prize in the time mentioned, it shall be assumed that the winner forfeited their prize which then shall not be transferable to any other party
  • In the above scenario, the video with the second highest view by 12 PM of the Contest end date will be chosen as the winner, and thus the correspondent contestant

    • Prize

  • “Prize” refers to the amount of Rs 50,000 (excluding taxes)
  • The Prize can only be collected through any type of account on Siddhartha Bank Limited
  • The video with the most number of views by 12 PM of the Contest end date will be chosen as the winner, and thus the correspondent contestant
  • The account has to be in the name of the Winner
  • No exception shall be made with regards to payment of Prize, it shall not me made anywhere other than a bank account on Siddhartha Bank Limited and also shall not be made on any account other than the Winner’s
